First Anomaly Observation

Decades after its establishment, an archivist from the Immortal Council, described as a luminous entity defined by a thousand sapphire triangles, recorded the first anomalies within the First Archive Vault: a microscopic, hairline crack on a sapphire data cube in an edge zone that curved slightly, introducing a feeling of dissonance and deviating from perfect crystalline geometry.
